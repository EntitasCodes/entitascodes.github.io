Amphibious Attire

  • These clothes strongly resemble a slippery-skinned critter. They also fit you suspiciously perfectly. When you Face Danger or Secure an Advantage +edge by using your swimming prowess, add +1 and take +1 momentum on a hit.
  • When you make a move +edge by using your jumping prowess and score a hit, take +1 momentum. On a strong hit with a match, your acrobatics seamlessly flow into your next action: make another move and add +edge.
