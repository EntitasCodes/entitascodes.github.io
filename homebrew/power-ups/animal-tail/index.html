Animal Tail

  • Your large, fluffy animal tail allows you to hover for some reason. When you make a move by temporarily suspending yourself in midair, add +1.
  • You may Strike +edge in close range by performing a dextrous tail-whack. If you do, reroll the lowest challenge die unless your result is a miss with a match. On a hit, take +1 momentum.
